Office 2024 Update Policy & Support Lifecycle

Understanding the update policy for Microsoft Office 2024 Professional Plus helps you plan for long-term use. As an LTSC (Long-Term Servicing Channel) release, Office 2024 follows a predictable update schedule that prioritizes stability and security over constant feature changes.

Support Timeline

Microsoft Office 2024 LTSC has a 5-year mainstream support period. Released in October 2024, it will receive mainstream support through October 2029. During this period, you'll receive monthly security updates that protect against newly discovered vulnerabilities, critical bug fixes that address stability issues, and non-security updates as needed for compatibility.

What Updates You Will Receive

During the support period, Office 2024 receives the following types of updates: security patches released on the second Tuesday of each month (Patch Tuesday), critical reliability fixes that address application crashes or data loss issues, and compatibility updates for new file formats or protocol changes.

What Updates You Won't Receive

As an LTSC release, Office 2024 does not receive new feature updates. The feature set is fixed at release. This is by design — LTSC users choose stability over constantly changing features. If you want new features as they're developed, Microsoft 365 (subscription) is the appropriate choice. For most users, the features in Office 2024 are more than sufficient for years of productive use.

How Updates Are Delivered

Office 2024 updates are delivered through Windows Update (automatic), Microsoft Update Catalog (manual download), Windows Server Update Services (WSUS) for enterprise environments, and Microsoft Configuration Manager for managed deployments. By default, updates are downloaded and installed automatically through Windows Update, requiring no user intervention.

After Mainstream Support Ends

After October 2029, Office 2024 will no longer receive any updates. However, the software continues to function normally. You can keep using Office 2024 indefinitely after support ends — your files, features, and functionality remain intact. The only change is the absence of new security patches. Many organizations and individuals continue using Office versions well past their support end date.

Version Numbers and Builds

You can check your Office 2024 version by opening any Office app and going to File > Account. The version number shows your current build, and you can verify whether all available updates have been installed. Click "Update Options" to check for updates manually or configure update behavior.

Planning Ahead

With 5 years of support, Office 2024 provides a long runway for productive use. When you eventually need to upgrade, the next perpetual release (likely Office 2027 or similar) will be available.

Office 2024 Pro Plus — Complete Features List

Published December 3, 2024

Microsoft Office 2024 Professional Plus includes hundreds of features across 8 applications. This comprehensive list covers every significant feature so you know exactly what you're getting for $29.

Excel 2024 Features
